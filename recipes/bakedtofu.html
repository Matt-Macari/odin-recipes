Baked Tofu

Description

This baked tofu recipe is insanely easy and insanely delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. extra firm tofu
  • Your favorite teriyaki sauce, my favorite is Soy Vey.

steps

  1. Rinse the tofu and press out excess water for 30 minutes
  2. Cut tofu into thick strips about 1/2in by 1.5in pieces
  3. Marinate tofu in teriyaki sauce for at least 1 hour
  4. Place tofu on a baking sheet and bake at 400 for 30 minutes flipping halfway through cooking time.
  5. Let cool and enjoy!

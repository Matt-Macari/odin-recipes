Sauteed Kale

Description

This Kale is fast and easy you'll want to eat it every night. Plus it's amazing!

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches of kale
  • 3-5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rip the kale into bite size pieces
  2. Heat olive oil in a large non-stick pan
  3. Cook garlic until fragrant
  4. Add the kale and Salt and pepper and cook until tender

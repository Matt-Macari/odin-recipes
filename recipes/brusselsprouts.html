Brussel sprouts

Desription

These crispy brussel sprouts are easy to make and sure to be a crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • Brussel sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons of Olive oil
  • 3-5 cloves of Garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Rinse brussel sprouts and cut them in half
  3. Mince the garlic
  4. Add the brussel sprouts, garlic, and the rest of the ingredients to a bowl and toss until coated and transfer to a baking sheet
  5. When the oven is preheated bake the brussel sprouts for 25 minutes or until golden brown, toss them halfway through cooking time
